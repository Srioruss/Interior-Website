some history

- 1920

Founded in the early 1990s, our company began as a small partnership between two friends who shared a passion for creating beautiful and functional spaces. Over the years, the company steadily grew in size and reputation, thanks to their commitment to delivering personalized and high-quality design solutions for their clients.

- 1986

As the company continued to grow, they opened several offices across the country and even expanded internationally, taking on projects in Europe and Asia. Today, the company is one of the most respected names in the interior design industry, known for their innovative approach to design, attention to detail, and unparalleled customer service.

Antilia

12.09.2010

Construction of Antilia started in 2004 and it took over six years for this 27 storey massive mansion to be completed. In 2010 the building was ready.However apparently due to some vastu dosha, the Ambanis hesitated to move in immediately. They were then residing at the ancestral home Sea Winds at Cuffe Parade. It was finally only in 2011 that the Ambanis finally moved into Antilia. An elaborate 10 day griha pravesh puja ceremony was conducted in the house, by top priests, after which the family moved in.

Burj Khalifa

06.01.2004

The design of the Burj Khalifa building is a Y-shaped structural system that replicates a desert flower called hymenocallis. The building's exterior cladding, or curtain wall, is a combination of textured stainless steel, glass panels, and aluminum.
